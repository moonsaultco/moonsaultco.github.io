Company Search

All Companies Massive Upside Rocketship Potential Unicorn
Massive Upside companies are early stage and offer the highest compensation potential due to the equity. They are typically Series A or B, so they are still generous with options and have low strike prices. You can have more impact on strategy, wear multiple hats, and try new things. Cultures are more casual and you need to be comfortable with less structure and rapid change.
Rocketship companies are mid-stage and growing very rapidly. They are well past business model risk and are focused on developing structure to repeat what’s working and scale. They are well funded and are likely to have a positive outcome (IPO or acquisition), but are generally well known so the competition for jobs is higher.
Potential Unicorn companies are late stage, but still pre-IPO, and have the chance to become the next Facebook. However, they have not reached their full potential yet, so you can still participate in their growth. They are well funded and have hundreds of employees, so there is more structure, greater resources, and your role is likely to be very specialized. They are the most competitive to join, but the brand value on your resume is enormous. They have the best chance of a positive outcome (generally an IPO at this size), but you’ll have a smaller slice of equity, so the payoff might be less.
